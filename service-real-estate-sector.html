Real Estate Sector

The real estate industry is one of the most dynamic and constantly evolving sectors in the UAE and has contributed significantly to the country’s economic growth. Like with any industry, the real estate sector also presents unique legal complexities that must be resolved with utmost care and professionalism; which is why Mahbub Hossain and Associates LLB has assembled a diverse team of solicitors in Dubai specializing in real estate law.

Our team of expert legal consultants assists clients with real-time, practical, and pragmatic solutions to navigate the legalities involved in the real estate industry, representing a variety of clients in the trade. We support banks and financial institutions, developers, landlords and tenants, contractors, regulators, and government bodies, aiding in real estate transactions and resolving disputes with special prowess in claim settlements, adjudication, and court action.

With years of experience in the field, our Corporate Lawyers rely on their comprehension and the latest real estate insights to foresee and overcome legal issues on behalf of our clients, responding to and solving affairs as and when they arise.
