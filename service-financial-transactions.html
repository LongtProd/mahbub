Financial Transactions

The UAE’s banking and finance sector is a dynamic and constantly evolving industry. Having worked for years with many esteemed clients in this industry, our solicitors in Dubai have the expertise and insights to aid clients in understanding and utilizing the new-age financial products, technologies, and regulatory frameworks.

Our integrated services assist our clients on matters pertaining to subjects from asset finance and corporate lending, to financial regulations and compliance. Our legal consultants also assist with financial problems, dispute resolutions, enforcements, corporate governance, bankruptcy, fraud, and financial crimes. Additionally, Mahbub Hossain and Associates LLB provide expert and practical advice on topics such as shipping finance among others.

Our finance lawyers represent clients locally in the UAE courts as well as DIFC courts, ensuring compliance with the laws of relevant jurisdictions. Supported and assisted by other practice areas within the firm, our financial law services are comprehensive and time-focused.
